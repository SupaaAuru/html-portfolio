Angie's Bad Memory Film List

Three films I can remember finding interesting or fun

Monster (2023) Kore-Eda Hirokazu

An emotional glimpse over the shoulder of 2 highschool kids and the people around them as they bump up against the restrictions and judgements of society.
Nothing is only as it seems.

Survive Style 5+, Gen Sekiguchi

I remember it being wild and beautiful, full of people living lives full of mistakes.

Worst Person in the World, Joachim Trier

Sometimes you need a story to reflect the rhythym of lifes frustrations and feelings of doubt. This film held them up and held them close.
